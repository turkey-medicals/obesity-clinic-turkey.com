Gastric Sleeve Surgery

Gastric Sleeve, also known as Sleeve Gastrectomy, is a popular weight loss surgery where a large portion of the stomach is removed, leaving a small sleeve-shaped stomach. This procedure is minimally invasive and helps with significant weight loss by restricting food intake.

Benefits of Gastric Sleeve Surgery:

  • Rapid weight loss with a minimal hospital stay.
  • Reduced hunger and cravings due to hormonal changes.
  • Significant improvement in conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and sleep apnea.
  • Minimal risk of malnutrition compared to other weight loss surgeries.

Gastric Bypass Surgery

Gastric Bypass surgery involves creating a small pouch at the top of the stomach and rerouting the small intestine to the pouch. This reduces the amount of food the stomach can hold and limits the absorption of calories, leading to significant and sustained weight loss.

Advantages of Gastric Bypass Surgery:

  • Long-term weight loss and a reduction in obesity-related diseases.
  • Proven effectiveness in treating Type 2 Diabetes.
  • Strong track record of success in weight loss and health improvement.
  • Minimal scarring and quick recovery with laparoscopic surgery techniques.

Gastric Balloon Surgery

Gastric Balloon is a non-surgical, temporary weight loss solution. A balloon is inserted into the stomach, where it is inflated to reduce the stomach‚Äôs capacity. This helps individuals to feel full with less food, promoting weight loss without the need for surgery.

Key Benefits of Gastric Balloon Surgery:

  • Non-surgical and minimally invasive procedure.
  • Quick recovery time with no need for incisions.
  • Ideal for those not ready for more invasive surgery.
  • Noticeable weight loss within weeks of balloon placement.
